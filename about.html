About MurphDunks

Welcome to MurphDunks — a market where you can predict for or against my 2025 athletic goals while supporting charity:

  • Whether I will dunk a basketball by the end of 2025
  • Whether I will run a sub 3:10 marathon by the end of 2025

The concept is simple: users support a side, with 100% of the pool going to charity.
