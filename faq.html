Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you doing this?

Three reasons:

  1. I want to support charity, specifically Misericordia Heart of Mercy which is a fantastic cause close to my heart.
  2. I'd like some fun/funny external motivation to accomplish two athletic goals I've set for myself.
  3. I'm trying to learn how to build simple full-stack applications.

How do the predictions work?

You can place a prediction FOR or AGAINST each of Murph’s goals. Winnings are split proportionally among those on the correct side of the prediction.

What happens to the money?

80% of all funds go to the winning side of each prediction. 20% is donated to charity regardless of the outcome. I will store the pots in a FDIC-insured money market fund until payouts.

Is this real money? How can I trust you?

Yes. Payments are collected by Stripe (a very common 3rd party payment platform). Hopefully, if you are predicting you know and trust me personally.

How will you handle payouts?

Payouts will be done manually via venmo. I will collect your venmo handle at time of bet entry. I will donate the charity portion to Misericordia once the events are over.

Why do you need my venmo handle?

See above.

Is this legal?

This platform is designed as a novelty prediction market with charitable donations and no guaranteed returns. It's not a licensed gambling operation.

How is success determined?

Success will be defined as:

  1. Marathon: Official race time via Chicago Marathon 2025 site.
  2. Dunk: clean dunk with a regulation women's ball on a 10 ft. hoop. Ratified by multiple witnesses and video.
