About MurphDunks

Welcome to MurphDunks — a personal prediction market where you can bet for or against two fitness goals:

  • Whether Murph will dunk a basketball by the end of 2025
  • Whether Murph will run a sub 3:10 marathon by the end of 2025

The concept is simple: users place bets, odds shift dynamically, and 80% of the pool goes to the winning side. The remaining 20% goes to charity.

Follow the journey, support a cause, and make it fun.

Payout Structure

When you place a bet FOR or AGAINST a challenge, your contribution goes into a shared pot. Once the event is resolved:

  • 80% of the total pot is split among users on the correct side, proportional to their bets.
  • 20% goes to a selected charity.

Example: If the pot has $600 total ($400 FOR, $200 AGAINST), and the goal is completed, the $400 FOR bettors win. The payout per dollar bet is: 

Payout = (80% of Total Pot) × (Your Bet / Total Winning Side Bets)
         = $540 × (Your Bet / $400)
